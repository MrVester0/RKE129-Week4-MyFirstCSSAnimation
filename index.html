Brownie

As soon as they don’t call brownies: brownie cake, brownie pie recipe, brownie cookies, brownie cake, brownie cupcake, brownie dessert. Therefore, to begin with, we will figure out what brownies are and how to cook brownies. Chocolate brownie is an American recipe. It is believed that the classic brownie was invented in Chicago, quickly gained popularity, and began to be made with various additives and toppings. Today you can find a recipe for brownie with cherries, brownies with cottage cheese and cherries, brownies with nuts, brownies with mascarpone, liquid brownies, a recipe with cottage cheese and raspberries, brownies with liquid chocolate inside, banana brownies, mint brownies. Indeed, depending on the recipe, brownies can have a different texture and appearance, hence the names chocolate brownie cake, classic brownie recipe (cookies), etc. To make brownies, you can use both ready-made chocolate and cocoa.

Ingredients Amount
Dark chocolate 100g
Butter 180g
Sugar 1 glass
Egg 4
Flour 100g
Walnuts 100g

Keep in mind that this is for 6-8 servings

  1. Melt butter and chocolate in a bain-marie and therefore mix everything.
  2. Whisk eggs and sugar with the mixer for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Chop walnuts with a knife and toast them a little in a dry frying pan.
  4. Add flour to the cooled butter and chocolate mixture and stir thoroughly.
  5. After that add eggs whisked with sugar and stir carefully until the mixture is homogeneous in colour.
  6. Turn on the oven and heat it to 200°C. The baking tray shouldn't be too big. Line the baking tray with parchment paper and pour in the mixture. Bake for 25-30 minutes.
  7. Cut the brownie once it has cooled down. Use a very sharp and thin knife.
